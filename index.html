About

Joanna

Joanna is a graphic designer who has varied interests in psychology, technology and art. She is scheduled to finish her studies in Design in Visual Communication at the University of Technology, Sydney in 2014.

Process

Her process involves research into the needs of the client and the audience, to meticulously produce works that are not only visually appealing, but clearly communicate a specified mood and concept.

Services

She has produced work ranging from identity, illustration, photography, report design, typesetting, book design and web design. I'm currently available for freelance opportunities so please don't hesitate to get in touch.

Library Newsletters

Work: layout, typesetting and imagery selection in line with the City's style guide

I was an inhouse designer for the City of Sydney Library for a contracted period. During this time, I was responsible for the majority of the Library's marketing collateral including their monthly newsletter series of which they had three; adults, youth and late night library.

Library Posters: Adults

Work: layout, typesetting and imagery selection in line with the City's style guide

I was an inhouse designer for the City of Sydney Library for a contracted period. During this time, I was responsible for formatting the majority of the Library's Adults, Youth and Late Night Library posters. These posters were produced in high volume, with the Library needing approximately 10-40 per month. I introduced a grid to such posters which were previously not present to accomodate for increasing event text and consistency in layout.

Sustainability Reports

Work: idea conception, photography, layout, typesetting, style guide

A group of UTS students and I were tasked with designing progress reports for the Sustainability Department of The University of Technology of Sydney. Striking differences in colour helped to differentiate one plan from another, while consistency in style was achieved throughout each 4 plans.

Website Landing Page

Work: idea conception, identity, logo, photography, layout

Landing page layouts were designed for a newly-formed search engine, especially for organic foods. I was tasked with the overall design of the brand, as well as the website. The rawness of wholefoods was reflected in rich vegetable photography, while, a modern feel was reached via simplicity of type and line.

Subselves Book

Work: idea conception, illustration/drawing, typesetting, layout

This was a personal experimentation involving subselves; the idea that each of us has several aspects of self that are unconscious. Drawings were shown to reflect these polarities and shadows in self. Typography was structured in such a way to be read from various angles, to further reflect other aspects.
