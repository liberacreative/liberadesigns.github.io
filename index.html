Services

I'm a graphic designer who is passionate about creating beautiful, relevant and meaningful design. I've worked within various industries but have come to specialise in working closely with educational/training companies, as well as, natural health and wellness companies.

I can help you:

  • Enhance your company’s image
  • Clearly promote and visually express your company’s values
  • Create visual communication that speaks to your target audience and engages them

I create:

  • Publication design and formatting
  • Branding and marketing collateral such as brochures, signage, etc
  • Web design, EDMs and digital banners
  • And more

ABOUT ME

I’m a strong advocate of natural health products, having personally reaped many benefits from the use of such products for over four years. Being a natural health consumer has made me especially attuned to the needs of the market, effectively allowing me to communicate to them via my designs.

Interests

I've long held a passion for translating meaningful ideas into visual form. I have a strong interest in working with natural companies and educational organisations that genuinely further the growth of individuals, having personally experienced their value to my own wellbeing.

Experience

I hold a Bachelors Degree in Arts and Science (Psychology) from the University of Sydney, and a Bachelors Degree in Design in Visual Communication (Advertising submajor), from the University of Technology, Sydney.
