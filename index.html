ABOUT ME

I'm a graphic designer who has an innate capacity for visual communication. My conceptual approach brings the abstract into clear, concrete expression. I'm very meticulous when it comes to details. Careful to ensure that design conveys the right mood and impression.

Interests

I'm passionate about translating meaningful ideas and ideals into visual form. In my personal works, I have a particular interest in exploring consciousness, self and psychology, as well as, bridging the gap between subjective and objective communication.

Experience

I hold a Bachelors Degree in Arts and Science (Psychology) from the University of Sydney, and a Bachelors Degree in Design in Visual Communication, with an Advertising submajor, from the University of Technology, Sydney. You can view my resume here.
