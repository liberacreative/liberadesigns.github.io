The Problem

  • I was tasked with creating a visual identity and landing page design for a conceptual website which, aimed to serve as a search and price comparison site purely for organic products.
  • The creators wanted to place emphasis on concepts such as "organic", "fresh", "warm", "natural" and "genuine", as well as convey the site's fundamental functions via design.

The Solution

  • OLOOQ's logo makes reference to the site's primary function as a search engine via the 'q', which parallels a search icon. The logo takes on a circular frame when placed over imagery and appears to focus on what is underneath, further reinstating OLOOQ's search and find function.
  • Produce photography, a naturally-derived colour palette and modern typography gives OLOOQ a fresh, yet warm, rustic feel, true to the authenticity of organic products.
