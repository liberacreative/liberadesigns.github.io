Web Design

Zink & Sons

Zink & Sons wanted to update their site to modernise the brand for the present generation, while retaining a sense of their classic history. A clean, lighter look was paired with vintage elements such as gold and, grainy photography. A neutral colour palette of grey hues was also utilised to allow Zink & Sons's garments to remain focal points. On each page, emphasis was given to actions such as "enquiries" and "bookings" through large navigational grids, in order to streamline the business contacting process.
