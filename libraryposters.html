Event Posters

City of Sydney Library | Images drawn from Library stock photo archives

For a contracted period, I was tasked with formatting the majority of City of Sydney Library's event posters to be displayed at Library branches. Such posters were produced in high-volume, with the Library needing approximately 20-40 different posters per month to promote events for Adults, Youth and Late Night Library. In keeping with the high-volume nature of this project, I introduced a grid (i.e. structured type columns) to accommodate for large amounts of variable content. The grid essentially allowed for different amounts of event text and imagery to be slotted in quickly and consistently, in effect, maintaining the same professional structure for each poster.
