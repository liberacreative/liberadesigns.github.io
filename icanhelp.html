How I can help you

My story

I've long held a passion for art and design, and am particularly interested in translating meaningful ideas and ideals into visual form. I hold a Bachelors Degree in Design in Visual Communication from the University of Technology, Sydney.

My interest for natural health developed after undergoing chronic illness which prompted in me, a drastic lifestyle overhaul. I became very interested in restoring my health holistically and seeking to find, not only just “quick fixes” through pharmaceuticals with side-effects, but safe and sustainable solutions. During this period, I personally researched and tested several natural health products including organic or natural foods, vitamins, skin regimes, essential oils, posture enhancements, etc.

I continue to be immersed in a natural lifestyle to this day, and a repeat consumer who is familiar with the growing market. I have a strong interest in working with natural companies and educational organisations that genuinely further the growth of individuals, having personally experienced their value to my own wellbeing.

I create:

  • Marketing collateral such as brochures, signage, etc.
  • Branding and visual identity
  • Publication design and formatting
  • Web design and digital banners
  • Email marketing design (EDMs)
  • And more

I can help you:

  • Enhance your company’s image
  • Clearly promote and visually express your company’s values
  • Create visual communication that speaks to your target audience and engages them
